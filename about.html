About Us

Our story began with a passion for culinary excellence.

Our Story in Pictures

Our Beginnings

In the heart of the French countryside, our story began in this humble yet charming kitchen. It was here, amidst the crackling of the wood-fired oven and the aroma of freshly baked bread, that the first recipes were crafted. Every detail—from the stone hearth to the handcrafted wooden tables—carries the essence of a time when meals were made with love and care, shared among family and friends. This dedication to authenticity inspires us to this day.

A Cornerstone of Elegance

As our passion for fine dining flourished, we expanded to this elegant venue on a bustling city street. The blend of traditional French architecture and urban sophistication reflects our vision to create a space that inspires and delights. Every detail, from the warm glow of the lights to the intricate ironwork, invites guests into an atmosphere of timeless charm. This venue has become a beloved gathering place where unforgettable dining experiences unfold.

Where Tradition Meets Modernity

Venturing into the heart of the city, we embraced the challenge of redefining fine dining in this grand, modern space. With sleek interiors and intricate ceiling designs, the restaurant reflects our bold vision. Though the journey brought hurdles, from operational challenges to adapting to a larger scale, our dedication turned lessons into triumphs. Today, this space seamlessly marries tradition and innovation, offering a refined yet welcoming experience cherished by all who visit.

Dining Above the Skyline

Perched high above Tokyo’s iconic skyline, this restaurant offers breathtaking views of the city, from the glittering Tokyo Tower to the sprawling metropolis. Each detail exudes sophistication and exclusivity, attracting renowned guests from around the world. Here, exceptional cuisine meets an unparalleled setting, creating a destination where luxury and artistry unite. This chapter in our journey stands as a testament to our passion for excellence and our place among Tokyo’s finest dining establishments.
